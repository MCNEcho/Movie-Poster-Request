## Benefits of New Structure

### Visual Clarity
- Emoji icons (ğŸ”§ ğŸ”„ ğŸ“Š ğŸ–¨ï¸ ğŸ“§ âš™ï¸) make categories instantly recognizable
- Consistent visual language throughout the menu
- Easier to scan and find desired operation

### Reduced Cognitive Load
- Top-level menu has only 6 items vs 15
- Related operations grouped logically
- Progressive disclosure - advanced features hidden until needed

### Faster Workflow
- "Refresh All" combines 3 common operations
- Print operations grouped together
- Announcement operations grouped together
- Frequently used items at top level

### Better Organization
- Reports: Read-only refresh operations
- Print & Layout: Output preparation
- Announcements: Communication tools
- Advanced: Power user and maintenance tools

### Improved Discoverability
- New users can explore submenus at their own pace
- Power users can quickly access nested items
- Clear separation between basic and advanced features
